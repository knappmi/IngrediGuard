About IngrediGuard

IngrediGuard is a mobile application designed to help people with food allergies quickly identify potential allergens in food products and restaurant menus. Using advanced OCR technology, the app scans ingredients lists and menu descriptions to alert users to their specific allergens.

Key Features

  • Menu photo scanning with OCR technology
  • Personalized allergen profiles
  • Quick filters for common allergies
  • Restaurant menu database
  • Offline scanning capabilities
  • Allergen severity customization
Download on Google Play

How It Works

IngrediGuard uses your phone's camera to scan menus and food packaging. The app processes the text, identifies ingredients, and cross-references them with your personal allergen profile to provide instant alerts about potential allergens in your food.
